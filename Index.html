About The Scholar

Maulana Hafiz Abdul Sami Asim (Hafizahullah) is a renowned religious scholar and orator. His lectures, based on the Quran and Sunnah, focus on reforming beliefs, actions, and guiding practical life. His simple, effective, and easy-to-understand style is a prominent feature of his sermons. This app is a humble effort to bring his invaluable lectures to you.
